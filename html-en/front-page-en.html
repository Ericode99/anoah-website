How to Find Us?

We are easily accessible by foot from Zurich HB, approx. 10-15min.

Or by public transport, from Zurich HB with bus 32 direction Hermetschloo, get off at the stop Militär/Langstrasse, then about 3 minutes walk.

If you come by car, it is best to park in the parking garage Helvetiaplatz (Molkenstrasse 5, 8004 Zürich).
