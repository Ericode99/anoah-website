FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Dear friends of vegan cuisine, dear ANOAH guests. In the following we answer the most frequently asked questions and hope to give you a little security for your visit with us. We are of course happy if you send us any additions by mail.


How Often Do You Change the Menu?

We offer you a fixed vegan 5-course or 6-course menu, which always appears on the 1st Wednesday of each month. The 6-course menu also includes our signature dish "Purple Rain" which so many of you appreciate.


Do You Also Serve Ã  la Carte?

Since we don't want to cause any food waste, we only offer our menu because we can plan more precisely.


How Long Does a Dinner With You Take?

A dinner with us takes about three hours, but it depends on how fast you eat and drink.


What If I Have an Allergy?

If you have any allergies, it is not a problem, we can adjust our menus to suit you. It would be great if you could tell us your allergies when you make your reservation, so that our kitchen crew can prepare everything for your unforgettable evening.


Can I Come Already at 18.30?

You can make a reservation from 7:00 p.m. and should arrive at 8:30 p.m. at the latest, but you can see more details directly in the info window during the online reservation.


Are Dogs Allowed?

Dogs are explicitly allowed, no matter what size, but we would be happy if you briefly state in the comments field of the reservation whether you are taking your beloved four-legged friend with you so that we can reserve a table with plenty of space for you.
