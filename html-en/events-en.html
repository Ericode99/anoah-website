SWEET & SALTY

ANOAH Brunch

We dive into a new chapter and offer you a brunch for the first time on Easter.

We start with a package of CHF 49, which includes dishes chefs choice, Zopf and Gipfeli from BakeryBakery. In addition, you can add dishes à la carte.

You can expect a large brunch menu, all Plant based! Various freshly squeezed juices and homemade smoothies!

We are especially proud of our guacao-without toast, an avocado toast without avocado or our vegan egg benedict with sauce vollondaise! There is nothing that does not make the heart of a vegan beat faster. From sweet to salty, from freshly squeezed orange juice to Prosecco à discrétion, everything is there.

AVOS X ANOAH

Vegan Sushi Nights

Together with friends from the hotel management school we dare the project and conduct VEGAN SUSHI NIGHTS every second Sunday under the name AVOS x ANOAH, you can book now via their website!

At AVOS Plantbased Sushi, you'll experience sushi in a new way. Discover combinations, tastes and senses you didn't know before. We want to share our passion for good food with you. The best part - it's all completely vegan and gluten-free!

AVOS Plantbased Sushi was created the way the best things are created, through love. Love for sushi, love for vegan food, and love for each other. When you're a vegan and you want to eat sushi, it's not that exciting. Then when you're looking for gluten-free and vegan sushi, you're limited to good old avocado maki. That was the catalyst why we started making sushi ourselves. We experimented with creations, juggled ingredients and always presented our results on social media. The demand kept growing and the voices kept getting louder. People want more than just avocado maki! Finally, we decided that we would like to share our passion and joy with you and AVOS was born.
